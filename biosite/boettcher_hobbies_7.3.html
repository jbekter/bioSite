City of London City of Seoul Naengmyeon Recipe

One of Cecilia's favorite passtimes is traveling. Her favorite travel destination is London, England. She recommends trying the traditional English breakfast bangers and mash to anyone traveling there.

Cecilia's next travel destination is going to be Seoul, South Korea. Although she has lived in Korea working as an English Teacher, she is looking forward to spending time there as a tourist.

Cecilia is a foodie and loves trying new foods from different cultures. Her favorite dish is a traditional Korean soup called Naengmyeon which consists of a cold beef broth, noodles, brisket, scallions, and radishes.
